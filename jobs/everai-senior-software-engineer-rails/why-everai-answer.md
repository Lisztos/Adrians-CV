**Question:** After exploring our product, Candy.ai, and learning about EverAI, why do you want to join us?

What stood out exploring Candy.ai is the engineering surface underneath a deceptively simple product: real-time chat infra, generative media pipelines, personalization that has to stay consistent over time, and moderation that has to work at scale without breaking the experience. That's a harder systems problem than a typical SaaS backend, and it's the kind of challenge I want to take on next.

I've spent four years owning Rails backends end to end, scaling core services for 20,000+ users at Demodesk and building an AI-powered pipeline at Innovandio that automated a full day of manual work down to minutes. EverAI's growth means the bar keeps moving, and I want a team where craftsmanship and velocity both matter.

I'm also drawn to the ownership here: working directly with product on specs and estimates, reporting into the CTO, rather than being layers removed from decisions.
